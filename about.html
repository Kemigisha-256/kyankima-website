Our Mission & History

Kyankima Women's Cooperative was founded in 2019 by 47 dedicated women in Mukono District, Uganda. We are involved in small-scale agriculture, handcraft production, and community health awareness.

Our Mission: To improve the livelihoods of women and their families through sustainable farming, quality handcrafts, and health education while expanding market reach for our products.

Our Team

We are 47 strong women working together. Our chairperson is Nalwoga Judith. Every member contributes her skills in farming or crafting to support our families and community.
