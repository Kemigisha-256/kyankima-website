Community Health Tips

Monthly tips contributed by our three partner health workers.

Tip 1: Handwashing Saves Lives

Wash your hands with soap and clean water before eating and after using the toilet. This prevents many diseases common in our community.

Tip 2: Balanced Nutrition for Mothers & Children

Include vegetables, fruits, and proteins in daily meals to keep the family strong and healthy.

Tip 3: Importance of Immunization

Ensure your children receive all scheduled vaccines at the health centre.
