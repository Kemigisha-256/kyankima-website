Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where is Kyankima Women's Cooperative located?

We are based in Mukono District, Uganda.

2. How can I buy your products?

You can contact us via phone, email, or visit us. We also deliver within Kampala and Mukono areas.

3. Are your products organic?

Yes, most of our agricultural products are grown using natural methods without harmful chemicals.

Yes, we welcome bulk orders for events, shops, and organizations.
